User Data Deletion Request

Users can request the permanent deletion of their data directly from the GEAR app.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Open the GEAR app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Enter your security PIN (if prompted).
  5. Confirm the account deletion request.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Workshop information
  • Customer records
  • Vehicle and job history
  • Inventory and spare parts
  • Billing and invoice data
  • Cloud backup data
  • User preferences

Processing Time

Once your request is submitted, your data will be scheduled for permanent deletion. The deletion process may take up to 7 days to complete.

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, tax regulations, fraud prevention, or other legal obligations.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the GEAR app, you may request data deletion by contacting us.
